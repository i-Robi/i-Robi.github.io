MojoGroups

Staying in touch with groups of friends on the go

Disconnected groups of friends

Back in 2010, communication tools still didn't meet several goals, such as staying in touch and coordinating with friends in real time. Text messaging would be quick but unable to handle group conversations, and reaching people over email would take a matter of hours. As a result, it was hard for groups of friends to share bit of their lives and organize things on the go.

Enter MojoGroups

MojoGroups offers a mobile app and web service that enable simple, instant, and rich group messaging. It is now possible to plan a night out or just share updates and photos with close friends and family. Like SMS, it’s instant. Like email, everyone is in the conversation.

Novel interface

In 2010, smartphone interfaces were still in their beginnings, and we took particular care in crafting a novel interface that would use the small screen size as efficiently as possible. In particular, we invented a horizontally sliding interface that would triple the available width, and radial menus that would take advantage of the body's ability to remember muscle motion and direction.
