Master's planning at Stanford is a brain-teaser

To plan their master, Stanford students have to take into account the master's requirements, personal constraints and conflicts between class schedules. For that, they have to locate, retrieve, store, manage and process large amounts of data that cannot fit on a screen nor in their brain.

Enter Class Picker

We solve this information display and cognitive problem with an interactive data visualization that presents to the user all the relevant information at a glance, and that takes care of the constraint solving problem for him.

Appreciated by everyone

“I wish I had this app at the beginning of my Master!”, or “When do you guys release this?”
We understood during the several rounds of user testing that Stanford students would really like to use this application. We showed it to representaties of the CS Department, and the application was then avaiable on the official CS Department website, available to dozens of CS Master's students.
