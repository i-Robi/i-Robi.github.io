Ingresse

The first event finder and online ticketing platform in Brazil

Disconnected ticket buyers and sellers

Despite hosting a large number of ticketed social events, the ticketing market in Brazil is extremely inefficient: buyers and sellers have to overcome several physical obstacles and time limitations to trade tickets, and they have little to no information on events.

Enter Ingresse

Ingresse was founded in June 2011 with the purpose of optimizing ticket sales in Brazil. Ingresse connects ticket buyers and sellers in a transparent market, empowering ticket buyers with accurate metrics to evaluate events and ticket sellers with smart customer targeting.

Getting to the right solution quickly

The competitive landscape in the startup world asks you to get to the right solution as quickly as possible. Constantly iterating through the design process allows us to understand our users, generate ideas, prototype, test, and finally implement the relevant features in a short amount of time.

Prometheus, a modular & responsive framework

The young startup structure forces us to move fast and with limited ressources. To overcome these challenges, I created Ingresse's internal web framework. Being modular and responsive, Prometheus ensures the cohesion of the website, lowers the costs of feature development, and bridges the gap between designers and developers.
